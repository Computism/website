ChatT Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

ChatT is a messaging and calling app published by Computism ("we", "us"). This policy describes what information ChatT handles and how.

Information we collect

  • Account information — a username and optional profile details (such as a display name and avatar) that you provide when you register.
  • Phone number — if you choose to provide it, used to identify you to your contacts and to display caller information for incoming calls.
  • Messages and content — messages, media, and other content you send are stored on the decentralized Internet Computer network so they can be delivered to their recipients and synced across your devices.
  • Push notification tokens — a device token used to deliver notifications and incoming-call alerts via Apple and Google push services.
  • Location — only if you explicitly share your location in a chat; we do not track your location in the background.

Voice and video calls

Calls are transmitted through real-time media servers to connect participants. Calls are not recorded or stored.

What we don't do

  • No advertising and no advertising identifiers.
  • No sale of your data to third parties.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking.

How your data is stored

ChatT runs on the Internet Computer blockchain. Your account data and messages are stored in canister smart contracts on that decentralized network rather than on servers we operate ourselves.

Data sharing

We share data with third parties only as needed to run the service: Apple and Google receive push tokens to deliver notifications, and real-time media infrastructure carries call traffic. We do not share your data for marketing purposes.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at the address below and we will action the request.

Children

ChatT is not directed at children under 18.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted in the app or on this page.
