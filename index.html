About Us

Experienced Engineers. Cutting-Edge Platform.

Founded in the United Kingdom, Computism brings together senior software engineers with decades of combined experience across web technologies, distributed systems, and product engineering.

Since 2020 we have focused exclusively on the DFINITY Internet Computer — the world's first blockchain that runs at web speed and can host full-stack decentralised applications. We help clients harness this platform to build products that are secure, unstoppable, and future-proof.

We are a small, senior team. Every engagement is led by a director-level engineer, ensuring quality and expertise at every stage.

Services

From architecture and smart-contract development to front-end product engineering, we cover the full stack on the Internet Computer.

Canister Development

We build and deploy Internet Computer canisters (smart contracts) in Rust and Motoko, engineered for reliability, security, and upgrade-safety.

🖥

Full-Stack dApps

End-to-end decentralised application development — from on-chain backend logic to polished, performant front-end interfaces hosted entirely on the IC.

🏗

Architecture & Consulting

Helping teams evaluate the Internet Computer for their use case, design system architecture, and migrate or integrate existing products.

🔒

Security & Auditing

Code review and security auditing of Internet Computer projects, drawing on years of hands-on experience with the platform's security model.

Long-term engineering partnerships for teams that need sustained, senior-level delivery — roadmapping, development, and operational support.
